Capability Breakdown

01

Mergers and Acquisitions Advisory

Scope: Financial & Tax Due Diligence, Business Valuation, Transaction Structuring, SPA Review.

Whether navigating an acquisition or preparing for exit, we provide objective, data-backed scrutiny of financial health and historic compliance. We flag latent liabilities early, ensure tax-optimized deal structures, and provide definitive technical guidance to leadership teams navigating complex multi-party environments.

Business Outcome: Transaction readiness, risk isolation, and preservation of valuation capital.
02

Fundraising Support for Startups

Scope: Cap Table Structuring, Financial Projections, Investor Deck Vetting, Due Diligence Defense.

Raising capital demands absolute institutional confidence. We architect defendable financial models and sanitize internal controls prior to institutional scrutiny. Our role extends through term sheet finalization, ensuring founders do not inherit disadvantageous tax scenarios post-investment.

Business Outcome: Investor confidence, streamlined diligence timelines, and optimized equity structuring.
03

Direct Tax Advisory

Scope: Corporate Tax Planning, Cross-Border Tax Strategy, Assessments & Appeals, Withholding Tax Counsel.

Taxation is not merely a compliance burden but a crucial strategic driver. We provide bespoke interpretation of evolving tax legislation, securing advantageous rulings where applicable, and building impenetrable defenses during high-stakes revenue authority assessments.

Business Outcome: Quantifiable tax efficiency, mitigation of penal risks, and confident cross-jurisdictional scaling.
04

Indirect Tax & GST Advisory

Scope: GST Registration & Structuring, Input Tax Credit Optimization, Supply Chain Audits, GST Assessments & Appeals.

Indirect tax complexity can quietly erode margins. We conduct thorough GST health checks, identify ITC leakages, and ensure your supply chain is structured to minimize cascading tax exposure. Our team defends clients during GST scrutiny and departmental audits with meticulous documentation strategies.

Business Outcome: Compliant GST architecture, maximized ITC claims, and reduced litigation exposure.
05

Audit, Assurance & Internal Controls

Scope: Statutory Audits, Internal Audits, Risk-Based Auditing, Board Reporting & Governance Reviews.

Governance drives enterprise value. We execute comprehensive statutory audits adhering strictly to national and international standards. Parallel to this, our internal audit desk builds robust control frameworks that satisfy board-level oversight and institutional investor expectations.

Business Outcome: Unquestioned financial fidelity, operational legality, and fortified stakeholder reporting.
06

Regulatory & Compliance Advisory

Scope: FEMA & RBI Structuring, Corporate Secretarial, ROC Filings, Cross-Border Compliance.

Complete compliance lifecycle management across FEMA, RBI regulations, and corporate laws. We structure inbound and outbound investment to optimize regulatory compliance, handle secretarial obligations end-to-end, and ensure your corporate house is always in order — so leadership can focus on growth, not governance gaps.

Business Outcome: Zero regulatory exposure, clean corporate records, and investor-ready governance structure.
